Cristian Meo

Delft University of Technology - Mila.

Hi! My name is Cristian Meo, I’m a final-year PhD student at Delft University of Technology, supervised by Justin Dauwels and Geert Leus, and mentored by Anirudh Goyal.

I’m currently working on Generative Models for video prediction and model based RL downstream tasks. My current research focus is leveraging pretrained Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to learn Generalist World Models.

Along my academic career I obtained a BSc in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Torino and a MSc in BioRobotics at TUDelft cum Laude. I have also spent six months as a research intern at Mila in Montreal, supervised by Yoshua Bengio and Anirudh Goyal.

I am in the job market right now. To get in touch, write me an email at: c.meo@tudelft.nl
